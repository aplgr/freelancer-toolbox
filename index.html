Why this exists

Freelancing is a constant trade-off between rate, capacity, risk buffers, and opportunity cost. These calculators help you turn vague assumptions into explicit inputs and a number you can defend. Use the presets as a starting point, tweak them to your situation, and export the JSON if you want your decisions to stay reproducible.
